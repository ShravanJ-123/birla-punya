About Developer

Birla Estates is a top-notch real estate venture of Aditya Birla Group, known for developing world- class residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects in India. The company majorly operates in Bangalore, Mumbai and Gurugram NCR. In November 2024, the company was officially rebranded as Aditya Birla Real Estate. Birla Estate headquartered in Mumbai, is a renowned and well-established real estate builder that is based in Mumbai. It has its premium regional offices in Mumbai, NCR, and Bangalore. The firm is owned by Century Textiles and Industries Ltd., and K. T. Jithendran is the CEO of Birla Estate. Only a handful of people in India have not heard about Birla Estate. It is part of a subsidiary of the Aditya Birla Group, which is now a leading multinational conglomerate with diverse construction work. The Aditya Birla Group was established in 1875, and since then, it has launched many of its subsidiaries.
